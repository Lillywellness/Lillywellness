Frequently Asked Questions

What areas do you cover?

Lilly Wellness is based in West London and travels directly to your home. Travel outside West London within London is available for a £10 additional fee.

What does your therapist bring, and what do I need to prepare?

Your massage therapist brings a portable massage table and all the oils needed for your treatment.

To prepare for your session, please have the following towels ready as these are not provided:

• 2 large bath towels – one to cover the body and one to cover or support areas as the therapist works.

• 1 hand towel – for extra support (e.g. under the forehead, ankles, or knees) or to wipe off excess oil if needed.

We also recommend having a warm shower before your session — this helps relax your muscles and is important for hygiene. Try to avoid a heavy meal immediately beforehand.

Please also ensure you have completed the Client Health Questionnaire before your appointment so your therapist can tailor your treatment safely.

What should I wear, and is there privacy to undress?

You'll be given privacy to undress to your comfort level before the session begins. Most clients undress to their underwear, and full draping is used throughout so only the area being treated is uncovered.

How much space do I need at home?

You'll just need a quiet, clutter-free space large enough to fit a massage table — most living rooms or bedrooms work well.

What payment methods do you accept?

Cash or bank transfer are both accepted at the time of your session.

Do you offer any discounts for regular bookings?

Yes — book 5 sessions and your 6th session is free as part of our loyalty package.

What is your cancellation policy?

Please get in touch via WhatsApp as soon as possible if you need to reschedule or cancel, so we can offer the appointment time to another client.
